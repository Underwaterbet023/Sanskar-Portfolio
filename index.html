September 2, 2024

Smart India Hackathon in Gautam Buddha University

The Smart India Hackathon in Gautam Buddha University is a premier event that brings together students, developers, and innovators from across the country. This hackathon challenges participants to develop cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems, fostering collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving skills.


Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. It has proved to be extremely successful in promoting out-of-the-box thinking in young minds.


The hackathon at Gautam Buddha University featured over 100 teams competing across various tracks including software development, hardware prototyping, and AI/ML solutions. The event was organized over 36 hours of non-stop hacking, with mentors from industry and academia providing guidance throughout.


Key highlights of the event included:

  • 1. Problem statements from various government ministries and private organizations
  • 2. Workshops on emerging technologies and entrepreneurship
  • 3. Networking sessions with industry professionals
  • 4. Prizes worth lakhs of rupees for winning teams

The event not only provided a platform for technical innovation but also emphasized the importance of addressing real-world challenges through technology. Many of the solutions developed during the hackathon have the potential to be implemented at scale, creating meaningful impact across various sectors.

August 25, 2024

Hack & Chill Hackathon in New Delhi

A hackathon is a fast-paced event where teams of developers, designers, and enthusiasts come together to collaboratively create innovative solutions to real-world problems. It typically lasts a few days, with participants working on various projects under the guidance of experienced mentors. Hackathons often feature a competition format, where teams present their prototypes or completed projects to a panel of judges. The goal is to foster creativity, problem-solving skills, and teamwork, while also providing a platform for learning and networking among like-minded individuals.


The Hack & Chill Hackathon in New Delhi was a unique event that combined the intensity of competitive hacking with a relaxed, collaborative atmosphere. Held at a premier tech hub in the heart of the city, the event attracted over 300 participants from various backgrounds including students, professionals, and startup enthusiasts.


The hackathon focused on four key themes:

  • 1. Sustainable Urban Development
  • 2. Healthcare Innovation
  • 3. Financial Technology
  • 4. Education Technology

Teams had 24 hours to conceptualize, design, and develop their solutions. What made this hackathon special was its emphasis on both technical excellence and user experience. Mentors from leading tech companies provided guidance throughout the event, helping teams refine their ideas and overcome technical challenges.


The event also featured workshops on emerging technologies, panel discussions with industry experts, and networking sessions. Participants had access to APIs and tools from sponsoring companies, allowing them to build more sophisticated solutions.


The winning projects included a smart waste management system for urban areas, an AI-powered health diagnostic tool, a blockchain-based microfinance platform, and an augmented reality application for interactive learning.

The Future of Health Monitoring

I am proud to share that I secured 1st Prize at the Abhivyanjana Hackathon 2024, organized by Gautam Buddha University, for developing an innovative Health Monitoring IoT Device aimed at improving personal healthcare and preventive medical systems. This achievement reflects my passion for combining technology, healthcare, and real-world problem-solving to create meaningful and impactful solutions.


🚀 About Abhivyanjana Hackathon 2024

Abhivyanjana 2024 is a prestigious cultural and technical fest that brings together creative minds, innovators, and problem-solvers from diverse domains. The hackathon provided a platform to build technology-driven solutions under real-world constraints, encouraging innovation, teamwork, and practical implementation. Competing against several talented teams, my project was recognized for its innovation, usability, and relevance to modern healthcare challenges.


🩺 Project Overview: Health Monitoring IoT Device

Healthcare is rapidly evolving, and IoT-based health monitoring devices are playing a crucial role in shaping the future of preventive care. My project focuses on building a smart, real-time health monitoring system that helps individuals track their vital health parameters efficiently.


    🔹 Key Objectives of the Project

  • 1. Enable real-time health Monitoring
  • 2. Promote early detection of health issues
  • 3. Reduce dependency on frequent hospital visits
  • 4. Make healthcare accessible, affordable, and smart

The project leverages IoT sensors, microcontrollers, and real-time data transmission to track vital health parameters such as heart rate and body temperature. By enabling continuous monitoring and early detection of potential health issues, the system demonstrates how technology can make healthcare more accessible, efficient, and proactive.
